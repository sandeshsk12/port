My Pet Hooligan: A Deep Dive

My Pet Hooligan (MPH) is more than just a collection of 8,888 unique 3D rabbit avatars. It represents a community-driven Web3 gaming experience built on Unreal Engine 5, redefining player ownership and creative expression. Tied closely to the KARRAT Protocol, a decentralized platform, MPH empowers gaming and creative projects through its native token, $KARRAT.

Factions, Lore, and Identity

The MPH universe features a rich narrative. Players join the fight against Meta Zuck Bot, a malevolent AI corrupting the metaverse. They align with rebellious Hooligans and benevolent OG ZuckBots to reclaim the digital world. This saga unfolds through "The Key Saga", an animated series chronicling pivotal events and characters.

Factions

  1. Punk Bunnies: Defiant rebels with edgy style.
  2. Mutants: Chaos-seeking results of experiments.
  3. Playboys: Elite, stylish thrill-seekers.
  4. Rogue Jumpers: Adventurous explorers.
  5. Cypher Buns: Digital wizards and hackers.
  6. Bunners: Peaceful, nature-loving souls.
  7. Hare Raisers: Entrepreneurial opportunists.

These factions drive identity, rivalries, and alliances, fostering a dynamic community.

KARRAT Token

The $KARRAT token, central to the KARRAT Protocol, powers the MPH ecosystem and connects players and creators. An ERC-20 utility and governance token, $KARRAT offers multiple uses:

  • Governance: Participate in proposals and votes via KARRAT Improvement Proposals (KIPs).
  • In-game utility: Purchase content, upgrades, and seasonal passes.
  • Rewards: Earn through gameplay achievements and participation.
  • Marketplace transactions: Facilitates transactions in the MPH marketplace.
  • WinVote licensing: Access governance tools via $KARRAT.

$KARRAT allows holders to shape the MPH future through governance and gameplay.
