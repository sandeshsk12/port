My Pet Hooligan NFT

My Pet Hooligan (MPH) NFTs are 3D rabbit avatars that can be used in the game. MPH is centered around seven factions, each with unique characteristics, born from a rebellion against MetaZuckBot: Punk Bunnies, Mutants, Playboys, Rogue Jumpers, Cypher Buns, Bunners, and Hare Raisers. Holding an MPH NFT grants access to $KARRAT claims, future drops, and potential benefits like land ownership.

Utility of the My Pet Hooligan NFT

  • Access to FBX Files: Enables 3D animation of your Hooligan in the Immi app.

  • Exclusive In-Game Goodies: Unlocks items and perks unavailable to non-holders, with more features planned.

  • Playable Character: Allows you to play as your Hooligan in future game versions.

  • Central Role in Ecosystem: Positions you for upcoming drops and activations, such as "The Others" and "Karrot Juice."

  • Enhanced In-Game Earnings: Provides benefits over free-to-play players in future updates.

  • Rental Opportunities: Potential to rent out your Hooligan to other players in the future.

  • Land Ownership: Future possibility to own land within the game.
