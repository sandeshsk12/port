Airdrop

My Pet Hooligan (MPH) holders have been eligible for two significant airdrops: $KARRAT tokens and ZuckBot NFTs.

$KARRAT Airdrop: As early adopters of the KARRAT Protocol, MPH holders could claim $KARRAT tokens based on their Hooligan NFT metadata and a standard allocation. The first claim period began on April 22nd, 2024, with a total of nearly 50 million $KARRAT tokens distributed. The KARRAT documentation indicates at least one more claim will be available after 12 months12.

ZuckBot Airdrop: Hooligan holders received a free OG ZuckBot companion NFT for each Hooligan they held. The claim period started on October 29th, 2022 and remains open indefinitely345.
