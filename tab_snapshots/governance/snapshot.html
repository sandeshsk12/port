    Governance

    My Pet Hooligan (MPH) itself lacks a dedicated governance structure. However, it is closely tied to the KARRAT Protocol, a decentralized platform for gaming and creative projects that uses the $KARRAT token. $KARRAT holders gain voting rights within the KARRAT Protocol governance, thus indirectly influencing decisions related to MPH.

    $KARRAT holders participate in governance through WinVote, a platform enabling proposal creation, voting, and delegation of voting power. This community-driven approach allows $KARRAT holders to shape the direction of the KARRAT Protocol, which ultimately impacts the development and future of MPH. The KARRAT Protocol also features a Security Council responsible for urgent matters and routine upgrades, ensuring efficient operation while preserving community authority.
