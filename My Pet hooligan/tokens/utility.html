What is KARRAT and How is it Related to My Pet Hooligan?

KARRAT ($KARRAT) is a cryptocurrency that powers the decentralized gaming platform called the KARRAT Protocol. The protocol bridges art and technology, enabling creatives to build communities through gaming, AI, and entertainment.

While KARRAT is intended for use across multiple products, currently, My Pet Hooligan (MPH) by AMGI Studios is the primary game utilizing it. This association closely ties KARRAT to MPH in practice, even though KARRAT is not exclusive to it.
